Mahindra Thar

About the Thar

The Mahindra Thar is a rugged and powerful SUV, designed for adventure. With a bold design and capable 4x4 system, it's built to tackle the toughest terrains with ease.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbo Petrol / 2.2L Diesel
  • Transmission: 6-speed Manual / Automatic
  • Drivetrain: 4x4 with Low Range
  • Ground Clearance: 226 mm
  • Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Convertible & Hard Top Options
